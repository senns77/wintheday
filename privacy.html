PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: April 2026

Win The Day ("we," "us," or "our") is operated by Steve Enns. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you subscribe to our newsletter or visit wintheday.me.

WHAT WE COLLECT

We collect your name and email address when you subscribe to the Win The Day dispatch. We may also collect basic analytics data about how you interact with our website (pages visited, time on site) through standard web analytics tools.

HOW WE USE IT

Your email address is used solely to send you the Win The Day dispatch and occasional relevant updates. We do not sell your information. We do not share your information with third parties except our email service provider, who processes it only to deliver your emails.

UNSUBSCRIBING

Every email we send includes an unsubscribe link. You can also email us directly at steve@wintheday.me to be removed from our list. We'll process your request immediately.

COOKIES

This site uses basic cookies for functionality and analytics. No advertising cookies are used. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.
